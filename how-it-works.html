How Item Matching Works

Our intelligent matching system automatically finds potential matches when you report a lost or found item.

Matching Criteria

Criteria Points Description
Same Category +3 Item belongs to the same category (Electronics, Clothing, etc.)
Similar Location +2 Item found/lost in a similar location
Recent Date +1 Item reported within 7 days of your report

🔍 How It Works

1

Report Your Item

Fill out the form with details about your lost or found item (name, category, location, date)

2

System Searches

Our algorithm searches for opposite type items (if you report "lost", it searches "found" items)

3

Matches Displayed

Potential matches appear with contact information and match score

4

Contact Owner

Reach out via email to verify and claim your item

Example Scenario

You Report: LOST iPhone 13

Category: Electronics

Location: Library Block A

Date: June 10, 2025

System Finds These Matches:

High Match (6 points) - FOUND iPhone 13

Category: Electronics (+3) | Location: Library (+2) | Date: June 11 (+1)

Contact: admin@portal.com

Medium Match (5 points) - FOUND Samsung Phone

Category: Electronics (+3) | Location: Library Block B (+2) | Date: June 15 (+0)

Contact: john@example.com

Low Match (3 points) - FOUND iPhone 13

Category: Electronics (+3) | Location: Cafeteria (+0) | Date: June 20 (+0)

Contact: sarah@example.com

Tips for Better Matches

  • Be specific with item names (e.g., "Blue Nike Backpack" instead of just "Backpack")
  • Provide exact location details (e.g., "Library Block A, 2nd Floor" instead of "Library")
  • Enter the correct date when you lost/found the item
  • Add detailed descriptions to help verify ownership
  • Check your email regularly for match notifications
